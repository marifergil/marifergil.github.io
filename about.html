Hi, my name is María Fernanda Gil Leyva Villa, I was born and raised in Mexico city. I have a college degree on Actuarial Sciences and a master's degree on Mathematical Sciences from UNAM. I also graduated with a PhD on Statistics and Probability from UNAM where I worked under the direction of Ramses Mena. Afterwards I was a postdoctoral fellow at Bocconi University and University of Turin where I worked with Antonio Lijoi, Igor Prünster and Pierpaolo De Blasi. Currently, I am an associate reasercher in the Department of Probability and Statistics at IIMAS-UNAM.

Research Interests

  • Bayesian nonparametrics
  • Stochastic simulation
  • Combinatorial stochastic processes
  • Random probability measures
  • Stochastic Symmetries

Awards and Honors

  • Francisco Aranda Ordaz 2023 on Mathematical Statistics (SLAPEM and Bernoulli Society)
  • National Researcher Candidate (SNI-Conahcyt)
